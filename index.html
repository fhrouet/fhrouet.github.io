François-Henry Rouet

personal home page
I am a research scientist at Livermore Software Technology, an ANSYS company. My fields of interest are parallel computing, sparse matrix computations, and graph algorithms. I collaborate to the MUMPS and STRUMPACK projects.
Education & experience:

  • 2015-: Research scientist, Livermore Software Technology.
  • 2012-2015: Postdoctoral researcher, Scalable Solvers Group, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
  • 2009-2012: Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, INP Toulouse, France.
  • 2006-2009: M.Sc. and Engineering degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, INP Toulouse, France.
