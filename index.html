François-Henry Rouet

personal home page
I am a scientist at Livermore Software Technology Corporation, LSTC. My fields of interest are parallel computing, sparse matrix computations, and graph algorithms. I collaborate to the MUMPS and STRUMPACK projects.

Recent presentation: I gave a talk titled "A comparison of parallel rank-structured solvers" at the SIAM PP16 conference in Paris, in April. Here are the slides.
Education & experience:

  • 2015-: Research scientist, Livermore Software Technology Corporation.
  • 2012-2015: Postdoctoral researcher, Scalable Solvers Group, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
  • 2009-2012: PhD in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, INP Toulouse, France.
  • 2006-2009: MSc and Engineering degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, INP Toulouse, France.
